Player's Name : Jean-Claude

Player Backstory

A long time ago in a region far, far away... a young adventurer in quest for knowledge was ready to explore new worlds.

With his training at Hogwarts School of Computer Science and Mathematics, he knew the right spells and wizardry to defeat complex problems. And although he had very little experience with his magical knowledge, his determination and desire to learn would certainly help him overcome obstacles.

Whether it was the astonishing caverns of low-level programming, the magical universe of quantum computing or the grand cities of theoretical computer science, Jean-Claude was ready.

Between his adventures, he liked tabletop games, outdoor activities, spending time with familiy and friends or work out a mathematical problem.
